How to use LearnNews

This is a chrome extension to help users learn vocabulary from news website.

You can choose to enable/disable the chrome extesion in the settings popup.

The words you learnt are remembered, as well as which words you forgot, and quizzes will smartly be chosen with different levels of tests based on your learning behaviour.

No strings attached, we believe in casual learning, to learn the vocabularies that are used most in real life.

In the end, Happy Learning
