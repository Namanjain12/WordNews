How to use LearnNews

This is a chrome extension to help users learn vocabulary from news website.

You can choose to turn on/off translate settings in settings page.

LearnNews will remember which words you learnt, which words you forgot, and it will smartly pop up to show different levels of tests based on your learning behaviour.

THere will be a summary box at the end of each news article to tell you what are the words you learnt, and as well as a suggestion news for you to read.

No strings attached, we believe in casual learning, to learn the vocabularies that are used most in real life.

In the end, Happy Learning
